RAGDOLL CAT🐈 Ragdoll cats are known for their delicate, calm, and collected demeanor, as well as their
gregarious nature. Ragdolls almost have dog dedication to their owners, being affectionate.
MUNCHKIN CAT🐈 Because of a genetic mutation, the Munchkin has very short legs, which gives the cat a rather
long, low appearance. They are fun, extroverted,They are sociable and loving cats who make amazing family
pets.
PERSIAN CAT🐈 Persian cat is a lengthy cat breed distinguished by that round face and a short nose.They are calm
and obedient. Being gentle and loving comes so easily to them due to their delicate attitude. They enjoy
being fondled and comforted, as well as being in the company of their owners.
MAINE COON CAT🐈 One of the world's biggest house cats is the Maine Coon. It has a particular physical appearance and
useful hunting techniques, and it is known for being welcoming, loving, and dorky - they are not only
enormous in size but also massive in personality. The Maine Coon, like a dog, appreciates being around
its family and can act as a pack animal at times.
